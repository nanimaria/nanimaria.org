About Us

NANI MARIA SOCIETY is an NGO founded by Smt. Desai Linggi, in the year 2002 based at Cheta-II Roing, Lower Dibang Valley Arunachal Pradesh, India. Nani Maria Society was formed to support and assist women and children of marginalized tribal communities and areas in the eastern region of the state, Arunachal Pradesh: NMS is also engaged in various socio-economic, socio-cultural and educational programs at the dedicated tribal areas. It has given due emphasis on empowerment of women and nurturing orphan & destitute women and Children.

NMS has been implementing and extending various programs for the cause, through campaigning community awareness, need based vocational training, rehabilitation service for orphan and distressed women irrespective of caste creed or religion. Under this program, the NMCH undertakes to identify children victimized by Child Labor, human trafficking, and Child marriage.

What We Do

In brief, these are

  • Child Care Institute (CCI)
  • Nani Maria Children Home for the Children in Need of Care and Protection. (CNCP)
  • Specialized Adoption Centre (SAA) for the adoption of the children, where they identify the children victimized by abandonment and are left to be orphan.
  • After Care Home for Rehabilitation and social integration of young adults who leave institutional Child Care System on attaining 18 years of age till, they reach 21.
  • Elementary School “Nani Maria Kinder Garten School” which is resolute to provide a sound and quality education by cultivating in the students, good values, virtue, and discipline.
  • Currently she is the member of Lok Adalat, in District court Roing.
  • Member of Harassment of women at work place.

About The Founder Chairperson Of NMS

OBJECTIVES

  • To focus on promoting the survival, protection, development welfare and participation of women and children in a holistic manner.
  • To provide basic human needs like Shelter and Education for Orphan and destitute women of Arunachal Pradesh.

EXPERIENCE

Smt. Desai Linggi is a professional social worker and women & child rights activist hailing from Cheta-II Roing, Lower Dibang Valley Arunachal Pradesh, India. She did her Education from Convent School, Doomdooma and Don Bosco Bible School , Hijuguri. Thereafter, She Joined Divine Word of School as an Assistant Teacher.

In 2002, She Initiated a small Hostel of mid-size range Hostel Named “Nani Maria Hostel” As things started getting better and bigger, this paved a way for her to start a school of her own ani Maria Kinder Garten School” . This was her milestone and presently various activities for destitute women and orphan children are being taken up by the Nani Maria Society.

Asociations

Besides NMS, she is also associate with various boards and committees namely

  • Chairperson, Child Welfare Committee, Anini, Dibang Valley, Arunachal Pradesh from 2010 to 2014.
  • The former Cultural secretary of All Idu Mishmi Cultural Literary Society (AIMCLS) which has more than 14,000 members.
  • She has appointed in the Local Complaint Committee as per 6 (1) of Sexual Harassment of Women at Work Place Act 2013 for Lower Dibang Valley District consecutively for 3rd term, Member of Internal Complaint Committee, as per section 4 of the above act.
  • She is also the Cultural Secretary for Enjahlu Menda Women Empowerment Forum (EMWEF).
  • She been invited on various platforms as a resource person and speakers like Radio Talk, Break Through shows.
  • Currently she is the member of Lok Adalat, in District court Roing.

The work of Mrs. Desai towards the marginalized and underprivileged children and women has been appreciated by the Honorable Chief Minister of Arunachal Pradesh and the Governor of Arunachal Pradesh Shri B. D. Mishra.
